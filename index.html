About

Chris Elms Tech Services is a sole-practitioner IT consultancy based in London, offering hands-on technical support to musicians, producers, artists, and private clients who need reliable service from someone who understands their world.

With a background as a studio engineer working alongside some of the UK's most respected creative talent, Chris brings not just technical knowledge but an instinct for how creative professionals work — and what they need from the people they let into their space.

The Approach

Every engagement is handled personally by Chris — there are no subcontractors, no call centres, no junior staff. Clients can expect straightforward communication, respect for their time and privacy, and work carried out with minimal disruption.

Many clients work in environments where confidentiality matters — whether that's an active recording project, a private residence, or a business with sensitive infrastructure. That's understood, and respected, without needing to be asked.
